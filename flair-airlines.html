Flair Airlines

A simplified flight selection process that enhances information clarity, builds user trust, and drives conversions.

Role

In this solo project, I leveraged competitive analysis, user interviews, online surveys, and usability studies to redesign a key touchpoint in the mobile flight booking process for Flair Airlines.

INTRODUCTION

Context

This project was completed as part of a two day design challenge for Flair Airlines. The objective was to evaluate the mobile flight booking experience and redesign a key touchpoint to enhance simplicity, clarity, and accessibility while driving conversions.

STARTING POINT

The Client

Flair Airlines, Canada's leading ultra low cost carrier.

Flair Airlines is an ultra-low-cost carrier (ULCC) committed to making air travel more accessible and affordable for Canadians. With a focus on efficiency and affordability, Flair provides a no-frills travel experience, allowing passengers to customize their journey during the booking process with optional add-ons.

EXPLORATORY RESEARCH

Online Survey

Surveyed 46 frequent travellers who booked flights online within the past year to explore their experiences, expectations, and challenges with mobile flight booking.

INSIGHT #1

Survey respondents identified price comparison, luggage allowance, and transparency of fees as the top three factors influencing their flight booking decisions.

INSIGHT #2

20% of respondents felt mobile screens lacked a comprehensive view of relevant information, preferring the desktop experience instead.

INSIGHT #3

27% of respondents struggled to compare key information on mobile devices.

INSIGHT #4

20% of respondents perceived a lack of control and input confidence.
