SCBC

Social Media Coordinator | February 2019 – October 2022

Objectives

In February 2019, alongside a team of three other fellow church members, I helped establish the Instagram and Facebook pages for Surrey Chinese Baptist Church (SCBC) with three goals in mind:

1. To carry out the theme of our church ("Passing on the Torch") by engaging the next generation.
2. To connect with members of our local community by increasing our online presence.
3. To increase the sense of community and connection between members of our church.

Our target demographic focused on English-speaking young adults, aged 18–24, as users of Instagram largely fall into this group.

My Role

As one of the social media coordinators, I create weekly content such as graphics, photographs and videos for the SCBC Instagram and Facebook pages. At the beginning of each month, I meet with my team to discuss upcoming events, evaluate our engagement statistics, revise our outreach strategy, and create a posting schedule, in which posts are assigned to team members on a quasi-rotational schedule.
