Director of Web and Social Media | May 2020 – April 2021

About

The Interactive Arts and Technology Student Union (IATSU) is the student body of the School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) at Simon Fraser University.

The IATSU Executive Committee is an elected group of students dedicated to providing opportunities and events for SIAT students to grow, engage with one another, and get the most out of their academic careers and university experience.

My Role

As the Director of Web and Social Media, I managed the website and social media platforms of the Interactive Arts and Technology Student Union, and was the first point of contact for students and organizations with inquiries and relevant design opportunities to be advertised to students.

​​

During weekly executive meetings and monthly meetings with the Director of SIAT, I voiced student concerns and discussed strategies to foster the growth of a welcoming and inclusive community within the SIAT program. Over the course of my term, I worked with my fellow executives to develop several initiatives such as our design challenges and student spotlights to increase student engagement and help students expand their network by using IATSU’s social media platforms to showcase their work.

​

Additionally, I created several platforms for students to stay connected with their peers online, including the SIAT Discord server and SIAT Undergraduate Facebook group, where students can ask others for advice on courses, connect with classmates, and receive feedback on projects. These groups have been extremely successful since they first launched at the start of the Fall 2020 semester, with over 1200 students in our Discord server and over 400 students in our Facebook group as of 2022.
