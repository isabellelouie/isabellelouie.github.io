Community

I believe in building inclusive spaces where people feel empowered to connect, collaborate, and grow. Here's some of the community work I've been part of.

SAP INTERNSHIP

During my year-long internship at SAP, I had the opportunity to get involved in Employee Network Groups (ENGs) and community initiatives alongside my core design work.

SAP Canada Intern Committee

As the Operations Lead / Coordinator for the Canada-Wide Intern Committee, I facilitated weekly meetings, ensured Event Managers received adequate support and budget, and maintained close coordination with our sponsors and advisors.

In addition to these responsibilities, I organized four networking and social events for the Vancouver intern community!

d-shop Vancouver Tour Guide

Originally known as the 'Developer's Workshop', d-shop is SAP's very own maker space. As a tour guide, I showcased innovative projects and initiatives to external visitors, including international consular staff, university groups, and community organizations.

I also helped promote internal workshops, events, and ongoing projects, fostering a culture of creativity and hands-on learning within the organization.

UXxUX Conference 2024

As a volunteer at this 3-day UX conference, I helped welcome over 200 attendees — designers, engineers, product owners, program managers, and students. I supported 16 speakers by facilitating Q&A sessions and ensuring workshop materials were prepared and ready.
